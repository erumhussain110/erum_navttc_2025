Software engineering is the branch of computer science that deals with the design, development, testing, and maintenance of software applications

A Quality Assurance

A Quality Assurance (QA) Engineer ensures a product or service meets quality standards and requirements before release by identifying and resolving defects, and verifying functionality through testing and analysis.

Software Test Engineer

A Software Test Engineer, also known as a QA Engineer or Tester, is a professional who designs, executes, and evaluates software tests to ensure the software's quality, reliability, and performance, identifying and documenting defects to improve the final product.

Front-End:

A Front-End Developer focuses on the user-facing, or client-side, aspects of a website or application, creating the visual elements and interactive experiences users see and interact

Back-End:

A Back-End Developer works on the server-side, handling data storage , logic, and functionality that users don't directly see.
